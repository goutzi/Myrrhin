About Myrrihn

Where does the industry go next?

The maritime industry is the backbone of global trade — 90% of everything the world uses travels by sea. Yet the education sector serving it has struggled to keep pace with the complexity, scale, and ambition that modern shipping demands.

"We built Myrrihn because we lived the gap between what the industry teaches and what it actually needs. That gap ends here."

Myrrihn is our answer. An education initiative designed with practitioners, for practitioners. From commercial shipping operations to cargo risk and port logistics, everything we build is grounded in real experience and tested against the demands of the real world.

We're in the early stages, and we're building deliberately. If you believe maritime education should be better, we'd love to have you with us.
